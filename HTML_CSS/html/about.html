THE LABEL

Undersound is a music community born from music lovers for music lovers. We put on nice parties around London where we only propose the music and artists we really love and admire. No industry politics, no gimmicks, only pure love for music and club culture.

THE PARTY

We are constantly looking for new venue and new artists, our aim is to let the true underground scene kepp growing givings space at talented Djs. Our first party has been in 2012 and since then we always tried to improve the experience for our crowd.
